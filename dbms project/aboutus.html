About Us

SNU Wheelz is a bicycle rental service operating within the campus of SNU. Our aim is to provide convenient and eco-friendly transportation options for students, faculty, and staff.

Our fleet includes a variety of bicycles suitable for different preferences and needs. Whether you need a quick ride to class or want to explore the scenic routes around the campus, we've got you covered.

At SNU Wheelz, we prioritize sustainability and affordability. We believe in promoting a healthier lifestyle while reducing our carbon footprint.
